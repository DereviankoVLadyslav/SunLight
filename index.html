ABOUT SUN LIGHT

Reliability Through Experience and Global Partnerships

Sun Light LTD was founded in 2013 and began its active international trade operations in 2018. Since then, the company has been steadily strengthening its position in the domestic market while expanding toward the EU, China, and Asia.

We focus on practical business supply, transparent cooperation, and long-term partnerships across transport, industrial equipment, and energy-related products. In the near future, the company plans to broaden the range of Chinese goods and open a representative office in China.

Core Sales Areas

Cars and trucks, agricultural machinery and equipment, automotive parts, and hand power tools.

Energy Portfolio

Solar panels, battery storage systems, hybrid inverters, and generators for modern energy projects.

Trusted Suppliers

Partnerships include John Deere, New Holland, Volvo, Scania, VAG Group, Renault, and Toyota.

International Growth

Cooperation with Hengyang Global Power Technology Co., Ltd. and Ningbo Deye ESS Technology Co., Ltd., with China expansion planned.
